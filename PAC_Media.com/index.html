Your Questions, Answered

What is your video editing process?

Our process begins with a discovery call to understand your needs and goals. You'll then send us your raw files, scripts, and any other editing resources or your brand kit. We will design a unique editing style tailored to your vision, edit the video, and deliver the final product. We offer up to 3 rounds of revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction.

How long does it take to complete a video project?

The timeline for a video project depends on its complexity and length. Typically, it takes about 24-48 hours from receiving the raw files to delivering the final video, including revisions. Well provide a more accurate timeframe during our initial call.

What types of videos do you edit?

We specialise in a wide range of video types, including promotional content, VSL, social media content, event highlights, educational videos, and more. If you have a specific type of video in mind, feel free to discuss it with us during the discovery call.

Do you offer customised video editing plans?

Yes, we offer fully customised video editing plans to meet your unique needs. During our initial discovery call, we'll discuss your specific requirements, goals, and preferences. Based on this discussion, we'll create a tailored plan that includes the style, length, and complexity of your video, ensuring it perfectly aligns with your vision. Whether you need ongoing editing services or a one-time project, we can design a package that works for you.
